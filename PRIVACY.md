# Privacy Policy

Table Exporter does not collect, store, or transmit personal data.

## What the extension does
- Reads table data from the active tab only when you open the popup.
- Exports that data locally to files you choose to download.

## What the extension does not do
- No tracking, analytics, or advertising.
- No external network requests for data collection.
- No storage of user data.

If you have questions, contact the developer.
